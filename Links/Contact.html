Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

Do you deliver across Uganda?

Yes, we deliver to all districts in Uganda. Orders within Kampala are delivered within 24 hours, while up-country deliveries take 2-3 business days. Delivery fees vary by location and are communicated before order confirmation.

Are your products genuine?

Absolutely. We source all our products from authorized distributors and guarantee 100% authenticity. Every item comes with our quality assurance guarantee. Pre-owned items are thoroughly tested before sale.

Do you offer repair services?

Yes, we provide professional repair services for all PlayStation and Xbox consoles. Our experienced technicians handle hardware repairs, software issues, maintenance, and console chipping with genuine replacement parts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo and Airtel Money), bank transfers, cash on delivery within Kampala, and online payments. Contact us for specific payment arrangements for your order.
